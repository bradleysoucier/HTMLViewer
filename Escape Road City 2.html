Escape Road City 2 - Chase the Thrill

Buckle up for Escape Road City 2, the high-octane sequel to the thrilling car chase saga! Continuing the first part, this time the player will act as a daring getaway driver. You will navigate a vibrant open world filled with chaos, fast-paced chases, and relentless police. Your mission is to guide your car to escape capture and unleash chaos as you climb the ranks of the wanted. Can you outsmart the law and become the ultimate escape artist?

  • Stunning 3D graphics
  • Roaring engines & sound effects
  • Unique vehicles
  • Soar to dodge mid-air crashes

The Thrill of the Chase

The thrill of the chase is just the beginning! You have the chance to immerse yourself in a dynamic city where every corner holds a new adventure. In addition, you also have the opportunity to escape on foot to diversify your gameplay and tactics. The pursuers become more formidable, but so does your avatar in the game. Players still need to explore and conquer the game's collection of awesome vehicles for improving their experiences. The streets are filled with action, and you must navigate the chaos and build a reputation as the most wanted driver in town!

Controls Guide

  • Steer: A/D or Left/Right Arrow Keys
  • Accelerate: W or Up Arrow Key
  • Brake/Reverse: S or Down Arrow Key
  • Jump Out: E
  • Parachute: Spacebar
  • Interact: F
  • Scan: Mouse
  • Boost: Hold Shift
  • Reset: R
  • Sharp Turns: W + A/D
  • Dodge Traps: Time Spacebar jumps
  • Avoid Cops: Watch mini-map
